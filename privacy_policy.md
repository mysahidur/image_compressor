# Privacy Policy for Image Compressor - MB to KB

**Last Updated: December 25, 2025**

"Image Compressor - MB to KB" (referred to as "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
The App is designed to perform image compression **entirely on your device**.
- **No Personal Data**: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, such as your name, email address, or location.
- **Image Processing**: All image processing (compression, resizing, format conversion) happens locally on your device. Your images are never uploaded to any server or shared with any third party.
- **Offline Functionality**: The App functions primarily offline and does not require an internet connection for its core features.

## 2. Permissions
The App may request the following permissions to function correctly:
- **Media/Storage Access**: To read the images you select for compression and to save the compressed versions back to your device's storage.

## 3. Third-Party Services
The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services that collect user data.

## 4. Security
Since no data is transmitted to or stored on our servers, there is no risk of your data being compromised from our side. We recommend keeping your device secure to protect your local files.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
